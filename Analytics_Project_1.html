Discussing Model Strategy

Variable selection in determining a Movies gross income and determining the probability of having a recommended outcome by majority of the critics is done by creating an initial model and running it through a step-wise selection procedure. Models are based on:

  1. Full Model - Initial model is composed of conventional and online indicators
  2. Conventional Indicators- Initial model is composed of conventional indicators
  3. Online Indicators - Initial model is composed ofonline Indicators

Models are created using Generalized Linear Model (GLM) and selection of the best model are then assessed through residual measures: R-squared, Adjusted R-squared, MSE, MAD, AIC, BIC.

With this, two models for determining a Movies gross income and determining the probability of having a recommended outcome by majority of the critics is generated. They are then compared if Generalized Additive Model (GAM) will create a model with better predicting capabilities. Moreover, we will choose the best model that will best explain the variability in a movies gross income and determining the probability of having a recommended outcome by majority of the critics. This is determined by using a 10-fold cross validation procedure and comparing with RMSE, R2, MAE for our continuous response model and Accuracy, Kappa for our categorical response model

Conclusion, Model Creation and Objective Fulfillment

Using GLM, we have have developed these two models for determinining a movies gross income and explaining the probability of having a recommended remark.

As a preliminary analysis, we have devided the predictors into two categories, conventional indicators, and online indicators. We then created 3 initial models composing of both conventional indicators and online indicators, conventional indicators only, and online indicators only. After this, we ran each model through a Stepwise Selection Procedure to determine which of those variables are significant for our model.

In determining which of the 3 models well choose, we compare their MSE, MAD, AIC, and BIC and choose a model with the least value.

After selecting the top 2 models, we also cross validated it using a 10-fold cross validation procedure and ran their equivalent GAM models. Weve compared the effectivenes of the model using RMSE, R2, MAE for continuous variables, and Accruacy and Kappa Coefficient for categorical variable
